Read `src/App.tsx` and `src/index.css` fully, then produce a structured audit report under these headings:

## 1. Narrative & Brand Voice
- Is the hero headline instantly memorable for a DevOps engineer?
- Does the About section tell a story or just list facts?
- Are achievements quantified with real impact metrics?

## 2. Technical Credibility
- Does the skill card copy demonstrate depth, or is it generic?
- Are projects described with problem → solution → outcome structure?
- Are the chosen technologies framed with context (scale, complexity)?

## 3. Visual & UX Punch
- Are Tailwind classes and CSS animations used to their creative ceiling?
- Is there visual hierarchy that guides the eye through a story?
- Which sections feel flat or forgettable?

## 4. Conversion & Engagement
- Is the CTA copy compelling?
- Does the contact section inspire action?
- Is there a clear professional identity that sets Ghassen apart?

## 5. Priority Fix List
Rank the top 5 changes by impact. For each: the section, the problem in one sentence, and the ideal fix in one sentence.
